What is art?


  • Art takes diverse forms: songs, movies, poems, quilts, and more.
  • Its meaning is subjective, evoking unique emotions in each person.
  • Defying a universal definition, art is a personal experience for everyone.
  • Each piece invites a personal journey of interpretation and connection.

Vincent Van Gogh's The Starry Night


  • Vincent Van Gogh found solace in painting during his year-long stay in a mental asylum.
  • Despite battling mental illness, he created over 150 paintings during this challenging period.
  • "The Starry Night," one of his masterpieces, emerged from this prolific time.
  • A self-taught artist, Van Gogh developed his distinctive style in his last four years, drawing inspiration from impressionism and Japanese prints.
  • Tragically, on the brink of success, Van Gogh's life ended in suicide.
